I am an artist/designer currently living in Wilmington, NC where I’ve practically lived my entire life. After graduating NC State University with a degree in Art+Design in 2018, I moved back to Wilmington and am currently the production manager/graphic designer at Canvas Giclee Printing. I am passionate about utilizing my visual skills to uplift the world in a positive way, either through printing an artist’s work or helping a business accurately communicate their brand. Overall, I believe design can change the world and it is up to us to turn dreams into reality.
